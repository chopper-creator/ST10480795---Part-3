Our Programs

TechBridge Youth Initiative provides digital skills training programs designed to help young people gain knowledge and practical experience in technology. Our programs aim to prepare youth for careers in the digital economy.

Computer Literacy Training

This program teaches basic computer skills such as using Microsoft Office, internet browsing, email communication, and online safety.

Introduction to Coding

Students learn basic programming concepts and are introduced to coding languages used in website and software development.

Website Development

Participants learn how to design and build websites using technologies such as HTML and other web development tools.

Digital Marketing Basics

This program teaches skills such as social media marketing, online advertising, and digital branding for businesses.
