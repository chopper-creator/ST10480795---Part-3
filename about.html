About Us

TechBridge Youth Initiative is an organization that focuses on helping young people gain digital and technology skills. The organization was created to support youth who do not have access to technology education, especially those from disadvantaged communities. TechBridge Youth Initiative offers training programs in computer literacy, coding, website development, digital marketing, and basic IT support skills.

The purpose of the organization is to bridge the digital gap between young people who have access to technology and those who do not. In today’s modern world, digital skills are very important for education, employment, and communication. However, many young people still lack access to computers, internet resources, and proper training. TechBridge Youth Initiative aims to solve this problem by providing free or affordable digital skills training and mentorship programs.

The target audience of TechBridge Youth Initiative is mainly young people between the ages of 15 and 30, students, unemployed youth, and community members who want to improve their technology skills. The organization also works with schools and community centres to provide workshops and training programs that prepare young people for careers in the digital economy.

Mission

The mission of TechBridge Youth Initiative is to empower young people by providing accessible digital skills training, technology education, and career development opportunities that prepare them for success in the modern digital world.

Vision

The vision of TechBridge Youth Initiative is to create a future where every young person has equal access to digital knowledge, technology resources, and opportunities that enable them to succeed in education, employment, and entrepreneurship.
